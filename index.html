ABOUT

I have an educational background in Kinesology, the scientific study of human body movement. I have always been a curious person asking myself the same question but in different environments, why does this (insert object / entity) operate the way it does? This desire to understand the inner workings of a system found its professional outlet in the energy industry, where I worked as a natural gas technican.

During my time as a natural gas technican with Southern Company Gas (SCG), I was not only able to see why systems (in this case, natural gas meters or natural gas appliances) functioned the way they do, but I was able to directly build and modify such systems. From the amount of gas pressure to the small mechanical objects inside the appliances / gas meters, each parts plays a crucial role to make the system function safely and effectively. Eventually my tendacy of uncovering why and how systems function led me to web development, a very broad and exciting field with many moving pieces waiting to be developed and explored.

During my free time, I enjoy visiting new cities, spending time with friends and family, and visting important historical landmarks.

My main objective now is to put the skills I've been developing over the last few years to good use by making a positive impact on online users by building user-friendly applications that meet the high standards that users expect. My preferred programming language is JavaScript and preferred tech stack is the MERN stack , using the React framework to build frontend applications and NodeJS for backend development.
